COACHING NOTES
→Rotate ankle weights between wrist and ankle positions to vary stimulus.
→Wear vest for squats and lunges before pulling exercises.
→Donkey kicks + glute bridges = glute growth without a barbell.
→Hanging leg raises train core AND grip — double duty.
→Supinate (chin-up) grip hits biceps ~30% more than pronated.
→Listen to shoulder fatigue — reduce vest weight if joints feel strained.
